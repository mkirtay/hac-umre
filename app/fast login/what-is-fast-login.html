ABOUT FAST LOGIN

What is Fast Login ?

Fast Login is an authentication platform provided via mobile phone number. Fast Login is supported by GSMA under the ‘Mobile Connect‘ name.

As a result of the market research of GSMA Mobile Identity, the authentication platform with the mobile phone number located under the GSMA Personal Data program.
